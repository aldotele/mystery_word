The Rules

The purpose of the game is to guess one word that connects 5 other "hint" words.

  • You will have 100 seconds to think before writing your guess
  • If you already have a guess, you can write it before the countdown stops
  • There is only one winning word per round
  • The case does not matter: "example" will be the same as "EXAMPLE" or "eXaMple"
  • Good Luck !

About Mystery Word Game

Mystery Word is the English version of the Italian Game known as Ghigliottina, which is the final step of the TV Quiz Show L'Eredità.

  • The game was introducted in 2005 as the final step of the Quiz Show L'Eredità
  • It was invented by Italian author Stefano Santucci and television host Amadeus
