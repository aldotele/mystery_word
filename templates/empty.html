The Rules

Given five "hint" words, the purpose of the game is to find the unique word that connects to all of them.

  • You will have one minute to think before writing your guess
  • If you already have a guess, you can write it before the countdown stops
  • The mystery word is unique: there is a single solution.
  • The case does not matter: "example" is the same as "EXAMPLE"
  • Good Luck

About The Mystery Word Game

The Mystery Word is the English version of the Italian Game known as Ghigliottina, which is the final step of the TV Quiz Show L'Eredità.

  • The game was introducted in 2005 as final step of the Quiz Show L'Eredità
  • It was invented by Italian author Stefano Santucci and television host Amadeus
